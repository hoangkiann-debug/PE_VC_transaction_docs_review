# Entity and Diligence Data Layer

Use before clause review when target/company facts affect the legal or commercial position.

## Entity Fact Pack

Collect or verify:

- Accurate legal names in Chinese/English.
- Unified Social Credit Code / registration number.
- Registered capital, paid-in/subscribed capital, contribution deadlines.
- Current shareholders and ownership percentages.
- Beneficial owners / actual controller / holding platforms.
- Historical shareholder, legal representative, registered capital, and address changes.
- Equity pledges, freezes, enforcement, bankruptcy/liquidation, administrative penalties, business abnormality.
- Material licenses, IP, qualifications, and litigation.

## Agreement Cross-Checks

Compare entity facts against:

- Parties and signature blocks.
- Recitals.
- Capitalization table.
- Disclosure letter.
- Founder warranties.
- Conditions precedent.
- Articles/章程 and resolutions.

## Use in Review

- If a fact is only from external enterprise data, identify it as external/background.
- If a fact is in the agreement, identify it as agreement/disclosure.
- If not verified, mark it pending and ask for documents.

## Common VC/PE Issues

- Agreement names do not match registered names.
- Investment amount and registered capital/capital reserve do not reconcile.
- Founder has unpaid contribution or shortened contribution deadline risk.
- Equity pledge/freeze conflicts with transfer, pledge, or closing conditions.
- Recent capital reduction or shareholder change affects warranties.
- Holding platform/UBO structure affects founder control and permitted transfers.
